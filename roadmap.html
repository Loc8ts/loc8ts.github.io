Up next

5

What we're working on right now - coming in one of the next releases.

Movement guardrails

Up next

Movement

Only offer the movement types that actually make sense for what you have selected. If a batch has no stock, stock out, transfer and adjust are disabled - a flagged batch can’t take a stock in, and so on.

Tighter item & location detail views

Up next

Items & locations

Rework the "items at this location" and "stock by location" sections so information is easier to scan - less back-and-forth between name and amount - with the relevant movement actions available inline (and disabled when they aren’t possible).

Consistent link styling

Up next

Design

Unify how links look and behave across the app so it’s always clear what is clickable - fewer competing styles, and whole cards clickable where today only the title is.

Stock counts that submit as you go

Up next

Stock count

Optionally write counted rows the moment they are counted, instead of waiting until the whole count is finished - so people working with the data see the current amounts immediately.

More reliable backend-down screen

Up next

Reliability

Make the "backend unavailable" state behave consistently, so you always get a clear message when the server can’t be reached.

Planned

3

Firmly on the plan and up as soon as there's room.

Email notifications

Planned

Notifications

Stay informed by email about the stock situation, building on the SMTP support already in the app.

  • Warnings: low-stock alerts, expiry-close warnings and expiry-breached reports.
  • Custom reports: configurable per user - pick stock reports, locations or items and set your own schedule.

Safer concurrent admin edits

Planned

Admin

Add stale-edit protection when editing a user, so if two admins change the same account at once the second one is warned rather than silently overwriting.

Full internationalisation

Planned

i18n

Take translation app-wide: a per-user language that defaults to English, a language switcher, and number formatting that follows the chosen language rather than the browser locale.

On the horizon

7

Something we've set our sights on, but haven't started yet.

Realtime performance & hardening

On the horizon

Realtime

Live updates are a core part of Loc8ts. This work debounces refreshes, adds rate limiting and server-side filtering, and batches import broadcasts so the realtime features stay fast and dependable with many people connected at once.

Account & security

On the horizon

Account

Give each account more control over language and security.

  • Per-account language preference.
  • Two-factor authentication.
  • A view of active sessions with the option to sign them out.
  • Self-service account deletion for non-admin users, always keeping at least one admin.

Multi-item transfer ("stash") mode

On the horizon

Movement

A mode for moving many items at once: pick items from one or more locations into a temporary stash, then drop all or part of the stash into another location - instead of transferring one item at a time.

Higher item-quantity limits

On the horizon

Items

Raise the maximum quantity per item by a lot, so tracking things like liquids or bulk stock no longer bumps into the current ceiling.

Item & location analytics

On the horizon

Reporting

Graphs of how stock moves over time - totals in and out per item, and how items move through a location - with selectable ranges like week, month or a custom timeframe.

Bulk delete

On the horizon

Items & locations

Delete more than one record at a time for items, locations, categories and thresholds, with clear handling of the stock movements a delete implies.

Live user info for everyone

On the horizon

Realtime

Show live actor names and updates to every role - including read-only viewers, who benefit from realtime the most - without exposing sensitive account details.

2

Ideas we're still thinking through - the direction is there, the details aren't.

A central place for everything

Ideas

Search

A single /search entry point that combines items, categories, locations and available stock with filters for all of them - so questions like "how much of this item is at that location?" can be answered in one place, then drilled into deeper. Needs careful design, since it may let other screens show less.

Admin housekeeping tools

Ideas

Admin

A dedicated area for admin maintenance jobs.

  • Prune old activity history, with the option to back it up to a file first.
  • Clean up stored images.
  • Manage imprint and privacy-notice settings.
